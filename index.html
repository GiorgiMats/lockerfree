    F.A.Q.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Am I insured for the opening of my door?

    This does not depend on our locksmiths but only on your home insurance. We advise you to check the status of your insurance directly with them.

    Do you only work in Bordeaux?

    We operate in Bordeaux and its entire locality within a radius of 40 km. Do not hesitate to contact us to see if we can intervene in your locality.

    Are your prices always fixed?

    The prices mentioned below are indeed fixed, some more complex interventions may sometimes require the creation of a free quote. For example, the installation of a new security door will be done on request.

    How much does it cost to change a lock?

    Our locksmiths may need to change your lock, because of a damaged cylinder for example. In some cases, they will be forced to change the entire lock. The supply and installation of a single European-type cylinder will cost between €110 and €150.
